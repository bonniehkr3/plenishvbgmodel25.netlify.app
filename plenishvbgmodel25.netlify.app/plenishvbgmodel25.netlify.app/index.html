About Plenish Water

Plenish Water is a leading provider of high-quality,reliable water supply services in the Nairobi area.

At Plenish Water, we believe in providing not just water, but a commitment to purity, sustainability, and exceptional hydration. Based in Nairobi, we're dedicated to offering convenient and eco-conscious water solutions for homes and businesses across the region.

Our Story

Founded on a simple premise: to make high-quality, great-tasting water accessible while minimizing environmental impact. Our water refill services emerged as a sustainable alternative to single-use plastics. We also offer premium, branded bottled water for events and personal use.

What We Do
